MovieBreeze 2.0

Announcing a new and improved MovieBreeze

New features and faster

Thanks for being a MovieBreeze user. We've been working hard to add some big new features. We've re-engineered it to be FASTER. We enhanced the movie details, the watchlist, and added some inspiration. Check out the new features:

Improved Movie Details

  • Trailers - multiple movie trailers
  • Full Plot- What was that movie about? We explain the plot in detail.
    SPOILER ALERT!
  • Discussions - Reddit discussions about the movie, wiki link, search for an explanation of the plot, Soundtrack on wiki and Spotify

Improved Streaming List

  • Service Groups - Streaming services are now divided into groups:
    • Ad supported
    • Rentals
    • Purchase
    • Cinema
  • Prices - Now includes prices for rentals and purchases
  • Resolution - SD or HD

Watchlist Filter

"Which movies on my watchlist are on one of my streaming services?"

  • Filter by Service - Limit your Watchlist to certain streaming services.
  • Filter by Genre - Restrict your Watchlist to certain movie genres.
  • Sort - your Watchlist by your own order or in order by when you added it.

Inspirational Ideas

We'll have great links and articles on new movies.

Pardon Our Upgrade

When you upgrade to 2.0 there will be an upgrade process that can take a minute or 2.

Sorry about the delay but it will be worth it!
